Artwork Name: Twisted Skater
Date: October 5, 2023
Twisted Skater was an artwork I made for Inktober in 2023. It was the 3rd prompt “zombie” and I wanted to try doing an pose I’ve never done before. Since the prompt was zombie I wanted to do something undead and I got inspired by Frankie from Monster High with how her limbs tend to separate from her body. I thought having her be a skater would be cool little detail.

Detail Highlight:
I really wanted to focus on the separation of limbs here and the bite marks that infected her.
