About Me

The Journey

I’ve been drawing ever since I was little. My mom always said I was artistic like my dad. As I watched more cartoons and anime, I became interested in animation and graphic illustrations. During high school, I started making video edits and doing line-art and recolors of my favorite series. Over the years, I’ve met several artistic friends and always had the “Let’s start an art business” conversation in the air. Now that I’m in college with a minor in digital arts and multimedia, I’ve decided to make that conversation a reality.


To A Bubbly Future

As I grow my skills through drawing I plan to open up commissions and sell cute designs on phone cases, hoodies, journal, etc. My Bubbly Future starts here and these are big goals to achieve:
Have a profitable store
Do graphics for a video games
Create my own comic
Work on an detailed short animation
